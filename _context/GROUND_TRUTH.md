## Discrepancies worth flagging

1. **Roles & permissions (#28) are documented as a hard rule but not implemented.** Operator API routes are workspace-scoped, not role-gated. Anyone with workspace access can hit any operator route. This is the largest spec↔code gap found.
2. **Wallet passes (#11) and Svix outbound (#20) are code-complete but uncredentialed** (`PASSNINJA_*`, `SVIX_API_KEY` blank). They will no-op until secrets are set.
3. **`lib/stripe.ts` is a thin lazy Proxy client** — all authorize/capture/refund logic lives in the route handlers + capture cron, not the lib module.
